Guidelines and Precautions to follow for COVID-19 Prevention

As issued by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of India.

Wash your hands with soap and water regularly. If soap and water is not available ,use hand sanitizer with atleast 60% alcohol.
Avoid mass gatherings and crowded places.
Wash your hands before touching eyes, nose and mouth.
Cover your nose and mouth using handkerchief,tissue or elbow while coughing and sneezing.
Get vaccinated: When it’s your turn, get vaccinated

If you are experiencing symptoms like fever,cough or difficulty in breathing, please call the state helpline number or 24X7 helpline numbers of Ministry of Health and Welfare, Government of India and follow the instructions. Don't delay or overlook your symptoms. For more information visit the MoHFW website.

About Us

Sahay is an initiative which provides a platform to connect people who are willing to come forward and donate , and thus help people who are in need of essential resources while fighting and recovering from the unforgiving disease.During these tough and trying times, inability to find the resources or lack of funds, should not become a hurdle while saving a life, and thus our moto "Bridging the Gap" to enable the beneficiaries and benefactor to connect.
